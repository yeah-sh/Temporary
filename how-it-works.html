How It Works

LocalDeliv simplifies local deliveries by connecting communities with a seamless, eco-friendly delivery system. Here's how it works:

  1. Sign Up – Join our platform as a driver or a local business.
  2. Place Orders – Local businesses receive orders through our app.
  3. Get Matched – Drivers are matched with nearby delivery requests.
  4. Deliver Locally – Drivers pick up and deliver orders in the community.
  5. Track in Real-Time – Both businesses and customers can track deliveries live.
